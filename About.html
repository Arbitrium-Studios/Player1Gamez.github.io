About Me!

Hello! I am Destiny! This is about Zachary Danielson. He is a 17-year-old with high-functioning Autism.


He is the creator of Toontown Fantasy and this website, the author of The Star of Toontown, he runs the Player Zero YouTube channel!

He is working on creating an interconnected Multiverse with my roleplays, my stories, my videos, skits, & movies, and of course, video games called The Destiny Saga!

About Player Zero Studios

Player Zero Studios is Zachary Danielson's own company that he wants to have go big, like way up there with Google, Amazon, and the bunch except better than them.


What he means by that is that he wants to create his own video games, movies, search engine (maybe), publishing service, and everything, hence the part of the name "Omni". Everything will be less corrupt and if he discovers that they're becoming corrupt, he swears he will turn his company into a non-profit company and fire the corrupt employees as quickly as possible!

About Player Zero!

Player Zero follows the life of Zachary Danielson, a Water Elemental, Tulpamancer and the incarnate of the Greek Primordial goddess named Thalassa as he attempts to restore his once-forgotten spirit name as a household name among the rest of the gods and goddesses by getting big on YouTube!
